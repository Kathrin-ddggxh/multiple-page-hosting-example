🥐 We love croissants!

Oh, how does he love these croissants for his elevenses. Now sold in packs of four, pre-wrapped in plastic to avoid contamination. Yet still the best to be had in these parts. What a joy to watch him peel the flaky crust off in a long spiral of pastry; fishing for the crumbly string with keen lips before devouring the soft and chewy inside of this buttery crescent.
Thank you, delightful croissants!
